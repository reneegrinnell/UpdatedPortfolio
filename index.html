About me

Connecting people through tech.

I’m a full-stack web developer with a background in clinical psychology. Connection, collaboration, and creativity are all important to me; they drew me to study human behavior and helped me discover a passion for programming along the way. The intersection of these two areas of interest, psych and tech, is where my strength as a developer lies. My deep behavioral knowledge helps me conceptualize how people interact with each other and with technology. With this person-oriented mindset and my technical abilities, I love to create beautiful, meaningful applications that are equally intuitive to users and developers.

I’m always hungry to collaborate and stretch my skills. Do you have a project or position in mind for which I could be a good fit? Let’s connect!
